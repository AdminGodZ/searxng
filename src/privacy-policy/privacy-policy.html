PRIVACY POLICY

Your privacy is our top priority. This policy outlines how we handle your information, adhering to Switzerland's strict data protection laws.

1. Logging Policy

We never log your search queries, user agent information, or your unmasked IP address.
We utilize the built-in SearXNG rate limiter, which temporarily stores a SHA256 hash of a salted IP address for a maximum of 10 minutes, solely for operational purposes. You can review the rate limiter's source code on GitHub.

2. Cookies and Local Storage

We use cookies exclusively to enhance your experience, such as saving your preferences. We never use these for tracking purposes and we do not employ third-party cookies. You can view your saved cookies in Preferences.

3. Swiss Data Protection

This instance is hosted in Switzerland, a country renowned for its stringent data protection laws and regulations. We comply with these strict standards to ensure the highest level of privacy for our users.

4. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Any modifications will be clearly posted on this page. We encourage you to review this policy regularly for any changes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about data protection, please reach out to AdminGod at admingod.ch

6. Effective Date

This Privacy Policy is effective as of 2025-02-25.
