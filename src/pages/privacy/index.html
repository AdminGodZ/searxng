PRIVACY POLICY

This Privacy Policy applies to the website "priv.au" and all its subdomains (collectively referred to as the "Website"). This Privacy Policy outlines our commitment to safeguarding your privacy, details the information we collect, and how we use that information.

1. Logging Policy

We attempt to minimise any forms of data logging. We have a strict "no-logging" policy which maintains that we do not log search queries, user agent information, request timestamps, or any other personally identifiable information.
We utilise a rate limiter to protect the Website from abuse. The hash value of an IP address is temporarily stored for a maximum period of 10 minutes in order to protect the privacy of users. This hash is collected solely for operational purposes. We do not collect or log your IP address after this period expires.

2. Data Collection and Sharing

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information with any third party. Your privacy is of paramount importance, and we are committed to preserving it.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

Cookies and/or local storage may be utilised to provide specific services, such as storing user preferences. These technologies are used exclusively for enhancing user experience and are not used to collect or track personal information. Third-party cookies are never used on our Website.

4. Hosting Provider

The Website is hosted on servers provided by Vultr Holdings LLC ("Vultr"), a cloud infrastructure provider. Their privacy practices are governed by their own Privacy Policy. You may refer to Vultr's Privacy Policy to understand how they handle your data.
Please be aware that while we take all necessary measures to safeguard your data, the hosting provider may have access to technical data and logs related to the operation of the servers. These logs are primarily for network and system administration, security, and performance monitoring.

5. Server Location

The servers hosting the Website are situated within the jurisdictions of both the Republic of Singapore and the Federal Republic of Germany. By using the Website, you consent to the processing of your data on the server geographically nearest to you, as determined by your DNS Resolver.

6. Changes to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify or update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes to this Privacy Policy will be prominently posted on the Website, and it is your responsibility to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or comments regarding this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, you may contact Brock Vojkovic at https://vojk.au.

8. Effective Date

This Privacy Policy is effective as of 2023-10-30.
